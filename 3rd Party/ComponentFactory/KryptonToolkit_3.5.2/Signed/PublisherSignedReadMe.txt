Publisher Signed Assemblies
---------------------------

Assemblies in this folder have been digitally signed with the
Component Factory Pty Ltd publisher credentials.

Right click the assembly and select 'Properties', then select
the 'Digital Signatures' tab to examine certificate details.

If you are writing an application for Vista logo certification 
then you need to reference these publisher signed assemblies
in your managed application. The Vista logo requirements 
specifythat your application and all referenced assemblies are 
publisher signed.
